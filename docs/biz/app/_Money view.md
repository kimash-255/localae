# 🧩 1. WHAT YOUR PLATFORM REALLY IS (MONEY VIEW)

Your system is NOT:

* a marketplace
* a POS
* a delivery app

It is:

> **A transaction routing + settlement system for real-world commerce**

Every transaction passes through you in one of two ways:

* Digitally (M-Pesa)
* Physically (cash)

But either way:

> You always record, split, and enforce value.

---

# 🧠 2. CORE MONEY FLOW (MASTER MODEL)

Every transaction has **4 stages**:

---

## 🟢 STAGE 1: INITIATION (IN PLATFORM)

Customer selects:

* Product (A)
* Service (B)
* Event-based item/service (C)

System generates:

> **Transaction Record (TRX)**

Includes:

* Total price
* Vendor/service provider
* Rider (if needed)
* Platform commission

---

## 🟡 STAGE 2: EXECUTION (REAL WORLD)

Two possible flows:

### 💳 DIGITAL FLOW (M-Pesa)

* Customer pays inside system
* Money is received (or confirmed)
* System immediately splits funds

---

### 💵 CASH FLOW

* Customer pays rider or vendor physically
* System records:

  * “Cash received externally”
  * Full breakdown of who is owed what

---

## 🔵 STAGE 3: SPLITTING (LOGIC CORE)

Every transaction is split into 3 buckets:

| Actor             | Earns                    |
| ----------------- | ------------------------ |
| Vendor / Provider | Service/Product value    |
| Rider             | Delivery / transport fee |
| Platform          | Commission               |

---

## 🔴 STAGE 4: SETTLEMENT (WHERE YOU GET PAID)

This is where revenue becomes real.

---

# 🧩 3. HOW PLATFORM EARNS MONEY (CLEAR STRUCTURE)

---

## 🅰️ COMMISSION (MAIN INCOME)

Every transaction has a % fee.

Example:

* Transaction: 500 KES
* Platform fee: 5–15%

👉 Earned automatically in M-Pesa flow
👉 OR recorded as debt in cash flow

---

## 🅱️ DELIVERY / RIDE MARGIN

For logistics jobs:

* Customer pays: 300
* Rider gets: 240
* Platform keeps: 60

👉 This is your **second revenue engine**

---

## 🅲 SERVICE MATCHING FEE

For services (B):

* Small “coordination fee” per booking/request

Even if no delivery happens.

---

## 🅳 EVENT MONETIZATION (C)

Events generate:

* Vendor participation fees
* Featured placement fees
* Commission on event sales

👉 Events = **high-volume revenue spikes**

---

## 🅴 FEATURED LISTINGS (FUTURE SCALE)

Vendors pay for:

* Top placement in Explore
* Event visibility boosts

---

# 🧩 4. CASH SYSTEM (HOW YOU STILL EARN FROM CASH)

This is where most platforms fail — you won’t.

---

## 💵 CASH JOB FLOW

1. Customer pays cash to rider/vendor
2. System records full transaction
3. System calculates platform commission

---

## ⚠️ PROBLEM: YOU DON’T HOLD MONEY

So how do you get paid?

---

## 🧠 SOLUTION: “DEBT LEDGER MODEL”

Rider/vendor account becomes:

* Positive balance → platform owes them
* Negative balance → they owe platform

---

### Example:

* Cash job = 500 KES
* Platform commission = 50 KES

System says:

* Rider owes platform: 50 KES

---

## 🔁 SETTLEMENT METHODS

They pay you via:

* M-Pesa (primary)
* Weekly reconciliation
* Wallet deductions (future phase)

---
